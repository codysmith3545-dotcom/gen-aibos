# Land Intelligence Module Spec

## Business Problem

Identify mispriced or underutilized land opportunities where zoning, utilities, access, ownership, political timing, infrastructure, or entitlement potential create asymmetric upside.

## Core Tables Used

people, organizations, opportunities, projects, tasks, documents, messages, external_objects, agent_runs, approval_requests, workflow_runs, data_sources, verification_items.

## Approval Rules

AI may score parcels, create verification items, and draft outreach. AI may not send outreach, make offers/commitments, or represent legal/zoning/engineering/surveying conclusions as final.
